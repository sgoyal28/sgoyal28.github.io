About

Dr. Shreya Goyal is an Assistant Professor at the School of Computing and Electrical Engineering (SCEE), IIT Mandi. Prior to joining IIT Mandi, she served as the Chief Technology Officer (CTO) at Cellverse Pvt. Ltd., a deep med-tech startup focused on developing next-generation, human-relevant platforms for preclinical research. She has also worked as an Applied Scientist at Generative AI Innovation Center, AWS and as a Research Scientist at American Express AI Labs. Dr. Goyal completed her postdoctoral fellowship at the Robert Bosch Centre for Data Science and Artificial Intelligence (RBCDSAI), IIT Madras, and earned her PhD from IIT Jodhpur. Her research interests lie at the intersection of artificial intelligence, medical technologies, healthcare data analytics, and trustworthy AI systems.
